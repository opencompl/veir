    Operation, Block, and Region Nesting #

    Heterogeneous nesting propositions between operations, blocks, and regions, such as parents, ancestors, and parent paths, are defined in this file.

    The nesting API is defined in terms of the IRNode type, which is a sum type of OperationPtr, BlockPtr, and RegionPtr. IRNode.Ancestor is defined by the existence of an explicit IRNode.ParentPath witness.
