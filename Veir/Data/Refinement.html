    def isRefinedBy {w : Nat} (i i' : Veir.Data.LLVM.Int w) :
    Prop

    LLVM.Int is refined by LLVM.Int. i' refines i if its behaviour are a subset of the behaviours allowed by i. In particular, any concrete i' refines a poison i, but a poison i' does not refine any i.

    Equations
    • (Veir.Data.LLVM.Int.val v ⊒ Veir.Data.LLVM.Int.val v') = (v = v')
    • (Veir.Data.LLVM.Int.val a ⊒ Veir.Data.LLVM.Int.poison) = False
    • (Veir.Data.LLVM.Int.poison ⊒ i') = True
